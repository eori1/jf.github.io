Nanoflare Nextage

NANOFLARE racquets are renowned for their repulsion, but realizing their full potential can be demanding on the overall hitting sensation. Enter the all-new NANOFLARE NEXTAGE, the gateway to unhindered speed coupled with a softer feeling hitting experience. Tailored for intermediate players seeking increased speed and an exhilarating hitting feel to elevate their game

This latest addition in the NEXTAGE series follows the heady heavy ASTROX NEXTAGE but in a head light alternative. Incorporating NANOFLARE DNA, the NANOFLARE NEXTAGE features the Sonic Flare System, enhanced with new technologies. By utilizing high elastic modulus HT GRAPHITE at the top of the frame, it creates a soft hitting feel upon impact. The Connected Grommets at the bottom enhance shuttle repulsion. The addition of these two technologies facilitates a swift attack and give rise to an exhilarating hitting experience. Furthermore, the Wide Profile Frame is employed to reduce frame warp during swings, ensuring racquet face stability and enhancing repulsion. Overall, the shape and repulsive ability of the frame are precisely developed to enhance the speed performance of the racquet. The overall frame construction creates the exhilarating feel of this racquet, enticing players to jump into quicker rallies.
