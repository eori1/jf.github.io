Designed for power, Offers a balance of power and control, catering to both offensive and defensive styles. Nanoflare

Designed for speed, Emphasizes rapid swings and maneuverability, ideal for doubles play and attacking singles players. Lightweight and head-light, Delivers fast swings and quick changes of direction, but may lack power for some players. Good for players who, Want a fast racquet for aggressive play, prioritize maneuverability, and enjoy a lightweight feel.

Designed for control prioritizes precise shot-making and strategic gameplay through a unique frame structure and "emphasized shuttle-pocketing" design. Suitable for all-around players versatile for both singles and doubles, but individual models may lean towards one style or another. Good for players who value control, want a versatile racquet, and enjoy a unique feel and response.
