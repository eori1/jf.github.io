A New Dawn for Doubles

Astrox S/D

A new dawn is here for doubles players with the introduction of the brand new ASTROX 88 S and ASTROX 88 D. With defensive techniques evolving rapidly, scoring points has never been more difficult. The ASTROX 88 S and D models are designed to overcome this challenge. The release of the PRO model will be accompanied by models for every skill level, ASTROX 88 S/D TOUR, GAME and ASTROX 88 PLAY for beginners.

The ASTROX 88 S allows for decisive front court attacks, while the ASTROX 88 D breaks down defenses with back court attacks. New technologies are introduced in this 3rd generation ASTROX 88 S/D as well as enhancements in the ASTROX series acclaimed Rotational Generator System. Advancing the Rotational Generator System further, a POWER-ASSIST BUMPER was added to the top of the frame, the extra weight enhances power transfer to the shuttle. The newly shaped POWER-ASSIST BUMPER fits seamlessly onto the racquet, reducing air resistance.
